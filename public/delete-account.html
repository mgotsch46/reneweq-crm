Delete Your Account & Data — Deal Flow Pro

How to request deletion of your Deal Flow Pro account and associated data. Operated by RenewEQ LLC.

Deal Flow Pro is a private business CRM. You can request that your account and its associated data be permanently deleted at any time using the steps below.

How to request deletion

Email marisa@reneweqllc.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Delete my account". Include your name and the email on your account so we can verify your identity.

Your workspace administrator can also deactivate and delete your account directly from the Team section of the app.

What gets deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we permanently remove:

  • Your account login and profile (name, email, password).
  • The contacts, leads, and property records you own.
  • Your notes, tasks, uploaded documents, and photos.
  • Your call, text (SMS), and voicemail activity logs.

What may be retained

We may retain a limited amount of information where required to comply with legal, tax, or regulatory obligations, or to resolve disputes and enforce our agreements. Any retained data is kept only for as long as necessary for those purposes and is then deleted.

Timing

We process verified deletion requests within 30 days.

Questions? Contact RenewEQ LLC at marisa@reneweqllc.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
